You shouldn't need thousands of dollars to trade.
Unfortunately, it's extremely difficult for people to get decent returns on the stock market without a significant amount of capital. Here at guap, we wanted to find a way for ordinary people to be able to start learning and earning on the stock market.

We've created a simple, secure platform where you can predict whether a stock will go up or down and put a few bucks on your prediction.

If you predict correctly, you win your initial deposit back as well as a portion of the losers' pool, based on how much money you initially invested. Instead of measly +3% gains, you could be making over 50% in returns on a single investment.

Stock market immersion, social competition and high returns. What more could you want?
